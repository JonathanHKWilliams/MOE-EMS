Educational Impact in Grand Bassa County

1. School Enrollment Rates

Grand Bassa County: 78% enrollment rate (up from 60% last year)

Impact: A 18% increase in enrollment due to the construction of new schools and an enrollment campaign.

2. Student Performance in National Examinations

Grand Bassa County: 72% pass rate in national exams (up from 55% last year)

Impact: A 17% improvement in performance due to improved teacher training and educational resources.

3. Teacher Qualifications and Training

Grand Bassa County: 85% of teachers are qualified (up from 70% last year)

Impact: A 15% increase in qualified teachers due to ongoing teacher training programs.

4. Dropout Rates

Grand Bassa County: 4% dropout rate (down from 10% last year)

Impact: A 6% decrease in dropout rates due to retention programs and family incentives.
